Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to share information about road tripping in the United States. It will provide routes, activities, and other suggestions.

Audience

This site is for anyone who is interested in learning more about planning a great road trip. They will be able to access this site on computer and mobile, wherever they have an internet connection.
