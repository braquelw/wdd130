Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to share information on white water rafting on the Salmon River. It will provide links to touring companies, trip recommendations, a packing list, and planning information

Audience

This site is for anyone who is interested in learning more about white water rafting, specifically in Idaho, and more specifically on the Salmon River. They will be able to access this site on computer and mobile, wherever they have an internet connection.
